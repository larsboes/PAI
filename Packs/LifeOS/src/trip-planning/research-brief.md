# Trip Research Brief

Structure for what the `trip-researcher` agent should investigate. Reference this when delegating research.

## Research Categories

### Transport
- Train options (Deutsche Bahn — preferred mode for European travel)
- Departure times, duration, transfers
- Price ranges (Sparpreis vs. Flexpreis)
- Night train options for late travel
- Airport transfer if flying

### Accommodation
- Area recommendations relative to event venues
- Hotel vs. hostel options with price ranges
- Proximity to public transport
- If employer office exists in the city — backup option for working

### Weather & Season
- Weather forecast for travel dates
- What to pack based on conditions
- Seasonal events or closures to be aware of

### Activities
Based on user interests:
- Bouldering/climbing gyms nearby
- Hiking options (if weekend/free time)
- Tech meetups or co-working spaces
- Good coffee/working spots
- Restaurants (no specific dietary restrictions)

### Event-Specific
- Venue location and logistics
- Registration status and deadlines
- Agenda/schedule if available
- Networking opportunities
- Any prep needed (presentations, materials)

## Output Format

The agent should return structured markdown with:
- Tables for comparing options (transport, accommodation)
- Links to booking sites
- Specific prices and times (not ranges if avoidable)
- Flagged time-sensitive items (early bird deadlines, limited availability)
