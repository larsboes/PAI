# Trip Research Brief

Structure for gathering trip information before planning.

## Research Dimensions

### Transport
- **Flights:** Available routes, airlines, price range, times
- **Train:** Routes, duration, comfort classes, pricing
- **Car:** Rental costs, driving time, local transport
- **Within destination:** Public transit, walkability, taxis/rideshare

**Priorities for you:** Cost + schedule fit + ability to work/travel

### Accommodation Areas
- **Neighborhoods:** Where to stay? Safe? Near activities?
- **Hotel/hostel:** Budget options, good reviews, cancellation policy
- **Workspaces:** If you need to work (coffee shops, coworking)

**Priorities:** Reliable wifi, quiet, reasonable price, location fit

### Weather & Timing
- **Current conditions:** Temperature, rain, snow?
- **Travel implications:** Delays, what to pack
- **Seasonal events:** Conferences, festivals, crowd levels

### Activities & Events
- **What's there:** Landmarks, museums, restaurants, nature
- **Events:** Conferences (career), festivals, tech meetups
- **Work:** Any client meetings, work commitments while there?

### Schedule Constraints
- **Your commitments:** When are you free? Hard dates?
- **Destination constraints:** Office hours, event schedules, timezone
- **Buffer time:** Travel days, jet lag recovery

## Research Output Format

```markdown
# Trip Research: [Location]

## Dates
[Your available window]

## Transport Options
- **Option A:** [Flight/train, cost, time, tradeoffs]
- **Option B:** [Alternative, cost, time, tradeoffs]
- **Recommendation:** [Which fits your constraints best]

## Accommodation Areas
- **[Neighborhood 1]:** [Why it's good, price range, examples]
- **[Neighborhood 2]:** [Why it's good, price range, examples]

## Must-Do / Key Activities
- [Event or activity 1 with times/dates if known]
- [Conference/meeting with exact times]
- [Optional: thing you want to see]

## Weather & Logistics
- [Temperature range, rain, snow, impact on packing]
- [Timezone difference, jet lag note]

## Open Questions
- [What still needs to be decided]
```

## Anti-Patterns

❌ Research everything perfectly before deciding → analysis paralysis
✅ Gather enough to decide transport + dates, plan activities later

❌ Over-schedule every minute → burnout, no time for serendipity
✅ Block out major events, leave 20% of time unplanned

❌ Ignore your work constraints → burnout mixed with travel
✅ Be explicit: "Working 2 hours morning, 2 hours evening, free rest of day"
